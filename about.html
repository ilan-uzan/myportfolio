About Me

I’m Ilan Uzan a product/graphic designer, full-stack developer, and creative entrepreneur based in Tel Aviv. I bring ideas to life through a mix of sharp design, solid code, and a deep understanding of how people interact with technology.

With a background in Social Sciences and Mathematics, I approach problems from both a human and analytical perspective. I’ve worked across UX/UI design, front- and backend development, branding, and growth — giving me a 360° view of what it takes to build digital experiences that stand out and scale.

I’m driven by curiosity, obsessed with good design, and always looking for ways to bridge the gap between vision and execution. Whether I’m designing an interface, writing code, or building something from scratch, I care deeply about craft and impact.
